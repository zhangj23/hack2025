### Step 2: Download Images

1. Click on an image you like
2. Look for a "Download" button (usually in the top right)
3. Save the image to: `server/public/styles/`
4. Use a descriptive name like:
   - `retro-1950s-ad-1.jpg`
   - `modern-geometric-1.png`

### Step 3: Verify

1. Make sure images are in: `upcycler/server/public/styles/`
2. Restart your server: `cd server && npm start`
3. Refresh your browser - images should appear in the Style Gallery!
